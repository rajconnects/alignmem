## Recording Workflow

1. **Write script** -- refine to your natural speaking voice. Read it aloud twice. Cut anything that sounds scripted.

2. **Record screen captures first** -- terminal setup, dashboard walkthrough, Claude War Cabinet session, live capture moment. Get clean takes of each without narration.

3. **Record face-to-camera segments** -- hook, problem statement, War Cabinet intro, close, and CTA. Natural light. Look at the lens, not the screen.

4. **Edit in CapCut or DaVinci Resolve** -- lay down the voice track first, then cut screen captures to match. Add subtle transitions between face and screen. No flashy effects.

5. **Export 1080p** for YouTube and LinkedIn. Crop a vertical version (9:16) for LinkedIn native video using the face-to-camera segments.

## Notes

- Keep pacing unhurried. This audience doesn't respond to hype.
- The silence at 0:08-0:20 is intentional. Don't fill it.
- Screen captures should show real-looking decisions, not lorem ipsum. Use decisions that would resonate with a Series A-D CEO.
- Total runtime target: 3:30. Hard cap at 4:00.
